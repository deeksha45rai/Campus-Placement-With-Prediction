Resume Database

The system includes a database of student resumes, enabling employers to easily search and find qualified candidates.

Analytics

The system provides employers with data and insights on recruitment performance, such as application rates, candidate demographics, and more.

Student Resources

Offer a centralized collection of study guides, practice questions, and other preparation materials organized by subject and topic.
